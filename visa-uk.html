Eligibility & Requirements

To be eligible for the Skilled Worker visa, you must demonstrate a set of core qualifications and sponsorship credentials.

check_circle
Certificate of Sponsorship (CoS)

A valid job offer from a UK employer approved by the Home Office.

check_circle
English Language Proficiency

Proof of speaking, reading, writing, and understanding English (B1 level).

check_circle
Financial Maintenance

Sufficient personal savings or employer sponsorship for your first month.
