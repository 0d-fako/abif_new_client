Our Heritage

Elevating Global Mobility Since 1998.

Abif Global stands as a beacon of excellence in international travel and visa consultancy. We bridge the distance between dreams and destinations with a meticulous, luxury-first approach.

A Journey of Trust

Founded on the principle that global exploration should be seamless, Abif Global began as a boutique consultancy in the heart of London. Over two decades, we have evolved into a premier international facilitator, navigating the complexities of global borders with grace and precision.

Our story is not just about logistics; it's about the people we've helped find their new homes, start their dream careers abroad, and reunite with loved ones across continents. We combine traditional professional integrity with modern technological efficiency.
